Montserrat is one of the most popular fonts that is used out there.
It is a geometric sans-serif that features a unique style with a particular
underlined style. All the letterforms and letters are based on the
lettering and scriptures found within the Montserrat neighborhood
of Buenos Aires. This font is perfect to be used both in editorial
projects as well as corporate projects.
